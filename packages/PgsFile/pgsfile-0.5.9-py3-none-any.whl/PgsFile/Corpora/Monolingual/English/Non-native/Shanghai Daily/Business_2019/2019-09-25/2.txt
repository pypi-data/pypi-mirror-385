Delta region talent to be tapped 
2019-09-25
A PROPOSAL to strengthen talent collaboration and cooperation in the Yangtze River Delta region was announced in Shanghai yesterday.
Cross-region talent collaboration, communication, cooperation, education and training work will be conducted in the delta region — a talent highland especially in the manufacturing and digital sectors, according to industry regulators in Shanghai and Zhejiang and Jiangsu provinces. 
Shanghai has 39 universities, 25 higher vocational colleges and other organizations which contribute 65,200 professionals a year. With the city’s economic development and position as an international metropolis, Shanghai has rich resources in high-end talent covering automobiles, airlines, the Internet of Things, new materials and advanced manufacturing, the city’s economy and informatization commission said.
