Singapore deals 
2019-11-05
A TOTAL of 56 major projects were signed at this year’s Financial Summit of China-Singapore (Chongqing) Demonstration Initiative on Strategic Connectivity yesterday in southwest China’s Chongqing City, worth of combined 194.5 billion yuan (US$27.7 billion).
The projects involved many areas including green bonds, debt financing, cross-border logistics, education and tourism, in a bid to enhance financial cooperation under the initiative.
