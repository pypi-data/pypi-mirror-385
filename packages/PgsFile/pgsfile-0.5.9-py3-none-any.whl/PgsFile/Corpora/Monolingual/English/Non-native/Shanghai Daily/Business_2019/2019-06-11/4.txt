Shanghai stocks rally 
2019-06-11
Shanghai stocks rallied yesterday after the China Securities Regulatory Commission said it will guide more long-term funds into the market and strengthen capital market resilience.
   The Shanghai Composite Index edged up 0.86 percent to close at 2,852.13 points. 
   New East New Materials Co Ltd surged 9.98 percent to 15.76 yuan (US$2.27) while Zhejiang Tony Electronic Co Ltd jumped 7.59 percent to 21.98 yuan.
