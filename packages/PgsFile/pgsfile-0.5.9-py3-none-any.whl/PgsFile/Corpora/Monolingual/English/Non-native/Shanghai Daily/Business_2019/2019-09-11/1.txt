Shanghai index sheds 0.12% 
2019-09-11
Chinese equities stumbled yesterday, despite gains among agriculture, forestry, medical and tech shares.
The benchmark Shanghai Composite Index shed 0.12 percent to 3,021.2 points, while the Shenzhen Component Index dropped 0.37 percent to 9,964.93 points and the blue-chip CSI300 index ended 0.34 percent lower at 3,959.27 points.
Turnover on the two major bourses totaled 696 billion yuan (US$97.72 billion), shrinking from 716 billion yuan in the previous session.
Shares in agriculture, forestry, animal husbandry and fishery industries and medical firms performed strongly, with Shandong Denghai Seeds Co Ltd and Kunming Longjin Pharmaceutical Co Ltd hitting the daily 10 percent limit.
