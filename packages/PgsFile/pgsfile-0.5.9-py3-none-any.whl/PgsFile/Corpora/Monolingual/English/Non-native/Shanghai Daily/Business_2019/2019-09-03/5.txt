Weekly sales soar 
2019-09-03
Seven-day sales of new homes jumped to a new year-to-date high in the last week of August amid robust sentiment in outlying areas.
   The area of new residential properties sold, excluding government-funded affordable housing, jumped 49.1 percent to about 265,000 square meters during the seven-day period through Sunday, Shanghai Centaline Property Consultants Co said in a report yesterday.
