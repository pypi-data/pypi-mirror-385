AI body to share resources 
2019-12-02
AI companies in Shanghai and other Yangtze River Delta cities have set up an association to share resources and assist each other.
The Shanghai Artificial Intelligence Technology Association has over 300 member companies. It is based at the Dobe Silicon Valley AI (Shanghai) Center in Qingpu District. Founding members of the association include the leading cloud service provider Ucloud, AI English teaching app Liulishuo, Dobe innovation and technology Co and Shanghai computer software technology development center.
