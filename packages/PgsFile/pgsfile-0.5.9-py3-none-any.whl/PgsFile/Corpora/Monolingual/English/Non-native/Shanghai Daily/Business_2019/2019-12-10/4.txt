Better deal for women 
2019-12-10
Chinese women employees accounted for 43.7 percent of the total employees in 2018, up 0.2 percentage points from the previous year, the National Bureau of Statistics said in a report. The proportion of women directors and supervisors in enterprises stood at 39.9 and 41.9 percent respectively, increasing 0.2 and 0.3 percentage points year on year. About 83.41 million women were covered by unemployment insurance by the end of 2018, surging 62 percent from the end of 2010.
