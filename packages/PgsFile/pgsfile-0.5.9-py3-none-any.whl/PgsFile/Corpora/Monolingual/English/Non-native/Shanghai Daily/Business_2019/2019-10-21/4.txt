List of Chinese VR firms 
2019-10-21
A list of top 50 virtual reality firms in China was published for the first time at the 2019 World Conference on VR Industry.
   HTC Communication Co Ltd, electro-acoustic components manufacturer Goertek and the smart tech arm of Chinese video entertainment company iQiyi topped the list.
   Well-known companies in the fields of the Internet, AI and cloud computing also made appearances on the list, including Baidu, iFlytek and Inspur Group. 
   The list was produced by the Industry of Virtual Reality Alliance.
(Xinhua)
